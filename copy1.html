Cozyama: AI-Powered Mid-Term Stays for Digital Nomads

Cozyama is developing an intelligent platform that understands the nuanced needs of digital nomads. Whether you need a minimum 50 Mbps WiFi connection, a large desk with an ergonomic chair, or an apartment within a 10-minute walk of a co-working hub, our AI is engineered to find your perfect 1-6 month stay (or longer), right down to the last detail.

How It Works:

  1. Smart Profile Creation

    • Complete our intuitive questionnaire about your work and lifestyle preferences
    • AI analyzes your past bookings and liked listings from other platforms
    • Upload photos of your favorite workspaces for visual preference analysis

  2. Intelligent Property Matching

    • Our AI scans thousands of listings, matching them against your unique profile
    • Properties are scored based on how well they meet your specific criteria
    • Receive a curated list of accommodations tailored to your needs

  3. Visual Insights and Virtual Tours

    • AI-powered image analysis automatically tags amenities and features
    • Take AI-generated virtual tours to get a real feel for the space
    • Visualize how well each property aligns with your preferences

  4. Transparent Pricing and Booking

    • View personalized pricing based on your requirements and stay duration
    • Compare rates with local long-term rentals and short-term alternatives
    • Book your ideal stay with just a few clicks

  5. Continuous Learning and Improvement

    • Provide feedback after each stay to refine future recommendations
    • Our AI continuously adapts to your changing preferences and needs
    • Receive increasingly accurate matches with each booking

Be part of shaping the future of mid-term stays:

  • Early access to the platform
  • Opportunities to provide feedback and shape our features
  • Exclusive launch offers

Join us in creating a new standard for digital nomad accommodation - where every detail of your ideal living and working environment is understood and matched.
